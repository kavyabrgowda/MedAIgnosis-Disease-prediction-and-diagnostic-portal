Balanced Diet for Day 1

Your guide to a nutritious and delicious meal plan.

Overview

This meal plan provides a balanced diet with a variety of nutrients to kickstart your week. It includes healthy meals that are easy to prepare and delicious to eat.

Meals for the Day

Breakfast

Start your week with a nutritious breakfast combining:

  • Vegetarian
  • Masala Oats
  • Veggie scrambled tofu with spinach, bell peppers, and tomatoes
  • Whole-grain toast
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Scrambled eggs with spinach, tomatoes
  • Whole-grain toast

Lunch

For lunch, enjoy:

  • Vegetarian
  • Aloo Gobi Sabzi
  • Lentil and chickpea salad with cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, and a lemon-tahini dressing
  • Side of whole-grain pita bread
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Vegetable stir-fry with tofu, broccoli, bell peppers, and brown rice

Dinner

End the day with a satisfying dinner of:

  • Vegetarian
  • Palak Paneer
  • Vegetable stir-fry with tofu, broccoli, bell peppers, and brown rice
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Baked salmon with quinoa and roasted asparagus
Complete this delicious, protein-packed meal.
