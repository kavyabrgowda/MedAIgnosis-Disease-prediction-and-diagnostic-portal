Balanced Diet for Day 2

Your guide to a nutritious and delicious meal plan.

Overview

This meal plan provides a balanced diet with a variety of nutrients to keep you energized and satisfied throughout the day.

Meals for the Day

Breakfast

Start your day with a high-protein breakfast of:

  • Vegetarian
  • Oats and Besan Cheela
  • Sprinkle of flax seeds
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Smoothie with banana, spinach, almond milk, and protein powder

Lunch

Enjoy a nutritious lunch of:

  • Vegetarian
  • Bhindi Masala
  • Panchmel Dal
  • Quinoa bowl with black beans, roasted sweet potatoes, avocado, and a lime dressing
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Turkey and vegetable wrap with hummus, bell peppers, and baby spinach

Dinner

End your day with a light yet nutritious dinner of:

  • Vegetarian
  • Kerala Olan
  • Fluffy Quinoa
  • Paneer curry with mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, and cauliflower)
  • Side of whole-grain roti
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Chicken stir-fry with broccoli, bell peppers, and brown rice
