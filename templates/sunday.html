Balanced Diet for Day 7

Your guide to a nutritious and delicious meal plan.

Overview

This meal plan for Day 7 ends your week with a variety of flavorful meals that are nutritious and satisfying.

Meals for the Day

Breakfast

Start your day with a protein-packed breakfast:

  • Vegetarian
  • Whole-grain pancake with fresh berries and a drizzle of honey
  • Vegan Chickpea Omelette
  • Omelet with spinach, bell peppers, and whole-grain toast
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Omelet with spinach, bell peppers, and whole-grain toast

Lunch

Indulge in a filling and protein-packed lunch of:

  • Vegetarian
  • Mixed vegetable and paneer stir-fry with whole-grain roti
  • Panchmel Dal
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Salmon and avocado salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and vinaigrette

Dinner

End the day with a fragrant and satisfying dinner of:

  • Vegetarian
  • Aloo Beetroot Sabzi
  • Spicy Mushroom Pulao
  • Baked sweet potato topped with black beans, salsa, and avocado
  • Non Vegetarian
  • Roasted chicken breast with quinoa and steamed asparagus
